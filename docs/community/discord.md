# Discord

Join the **Cyber Dev Studio** community on Discord! This is the official gathering place for Nova2D developers and enthusiasts.

## What you'll find

- **Community support** — ask questions, share your Nova2D projects, get help, and learn from other devs
- **Development updates** — early access to new features, changelogs, and roadmap discussions
- **Showcase your games** — share what you're building with Nova2D
- **Collaborate** — find contributors, team up on projects, or just chat about game dev
- **Direct feedback** — talk directly with the maintainers and help shape Nova2D's future
