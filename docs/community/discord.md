# Discord

Join the **Cyber Dev Studio** community on Discord! This is the official space for Nova2D users and anyone interested in Love2D game development.

## What you'll find

- **Community support** — ask questions, share your projects, get help with Nova2D or Love2D
- **Development updates** — early access to new features, changelogs, and roadmap discussions
- **Showcase your games** — share what you're building with the community
- **Collaborate** — find contributors, team up on projects, or just chat about game dev
- **Direct feedback** — talk directly with the maintainers and help shape Nova2D's future
